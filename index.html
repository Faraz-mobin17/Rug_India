About Us

Rug India is a Manufacturer and Exporter of Indian Handmade Rugs since 1985, specialised in Custom made rugs in all kinds of Flatweave, Indo Nepalese, Hand Tufted and Hand Loom rugs. Our goal is to provide creative and practical design solution throughout the design build experience with over many exclusive collections offering a wide variety of unique textures, colors and designs.

We only employ skilled and adult artsian. No Child Labour is used in our industry.

All our rugs are handmade with perfection by our lovely craftsman driven to make a Great home. We believe in making each rug with Love to help bring serenity to any space keeping in mind the rug should stand the test of time and be loved forever. Every detail is carefully finished by hand before it leaves for its new home.

hand knotted
Flat weave rug are likely to be the oldest type of rug quality, which is created using a simple weaving technique that provide a beautiful detailed pattern and rich colours.

Yarns are woven directly on the loom resulting in lighter, more flexible and reversible rug as the pattern comes out the same on the both sides of the rug.

These are durable, easy to move, easy to clean and versatile for any room and is the perfet laid-back layer.

These can also made in natural material such as Jute, Hemp and Sisal.

hand tuffed
Kilim rug are generally flat weave rug woven with slightly different patterns and style but still display the same general weaving skill.

They contain wide ranges of styles and are example of amazing craftsmanship.

These are relatively inexpensive and do not shed as much as other type of rug.

hand loom
Hand Knotted rug are made using a technique in which the rug is woven by creating a knot on a specialised loom.

The yarn is tied by hand around the two neighbouring threads creating the knot on the surface of the rug.

This might take couple of weeks to weave a rug but the end result is worth it by far.

Each one is unique and a direct reflection of the weaver’s creativity.
